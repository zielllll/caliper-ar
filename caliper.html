Caliper AR

Measure real objects
with no reference card.

Uses your phone's motion sensors (ARCore) to recover true metric scale — the same mechanism as the Apple/Google Measure apps. Aim the on-screen dot at each end of the object and tap.

1

Point at a textured, well-lit surface and move the phone slowly for a second so it maps the space.

2

Aim the ring at one end of the object and tap to drop a point.

3

Aim at the other end and tap again — the distance appears in cm and mm.
